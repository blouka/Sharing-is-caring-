Charlie's Story

Charlie is 18, and has just finished his A Levels. He always dreamt to go to university and study Engineering. He is getting good grades in all his GCSEs and his A Levels. However, he lives in a household where income is very low, which means that he does not have the financial means to go and study at univeristy. Charlie will find a job and his dream of going to university will be over.
